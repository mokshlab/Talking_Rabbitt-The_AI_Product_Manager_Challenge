# Kill Strategy — Head-to-Head Sales Playbook

## Core Positioning Statement

> *"ThoughtSpot charges $25/user and keeps your data in their cloud. Tableau charges $75/user. Power BI requires DAX expertise. Talking Rabbitt gives you private-cloud conversational analytics at a fraction of the cost — and your data never leaves India."*

## Competitive Sales Cycle Playbook

### Step 1: Land with Pain (Week 1)
- Run a **15-minute live demo**: upload the prospect's own CSV → ask 3 questions → show instant answers with charts
- Frame it as: *"What took your analyst 2 hours just happened in 10 seconds"*

### Step 2: Expand with Compliance (Week 2)
- Present the private cloud architecture diagram
- Show: *"Your competitors are sending data to US cloud. You're keeping it in your VPC."*
- Engage the CISO/compliance team as internal champion

### Step 3: Close with ROI (Week 3-4)
- **ROI calculation**: 3-person analyst team × 40% time saved = 1.2 FTE reclaimed annually
- At ₹15L/analyst salary → **₹18L annual savings** vs **₹3-5L Rabbitt annual cost**
- **3.5x ROI** from day one

### Step 4: Defend Against Incumbents (Ongoing)
- If ThoughtSpot counter-pitches: *"Ask them about private cloud. Ask them about RBI compliance."*
- If Power BI counter-pitches: *"Ask your VPs to write a DAX query. Time them."*
- If Tableau counter-pitches: *"Ask them why they retired Ask Data."*
