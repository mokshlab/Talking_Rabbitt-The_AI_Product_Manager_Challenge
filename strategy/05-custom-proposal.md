## The Challenge

Poonawalla Fincorp manages a ₹25,000 Cr loan book across personal loans, business loans, and pre-owned car financing, serving customers across 100+ branches nationwide. As one of India's fastest-growing digital-first NBFCs, the company processes millions of data points daily from loan origination, collections, and customer servicing.

**Current Pain Points:**

1. **Analyst Bottleneck**: 3-4 data analysts serve 20+ VPs and managers. Average turnaround for an ad-hoc data request: **4-6 hours**.
2. **Compliance Pressure**: RBI's increased scrutiny on NBFC data governance means all analytical tools must ensure data doesn't leave Indian infrastructure. Current BI tools (Power BI) route data through Microsoft's global cloud.
3. **Decision Lag**: Branch managers need real-time collection efficiency metrics but depend on weekly Excel reports sent by the central analytics team.
4. **Cost Escalation**: Enterprise Tableau licenses for 25 users cost **₹15-20L/year** — yet only 5 users can actually build dashboards.
